INTERNAL MEMO — CHOW NETWORK EXPANSION, EYES ONLY

Mr. Chow is expanding operations to Macau. Leslie Chow Enterprises has opened
the Macau Lotus Facility, a new production site on the Cotai Strip.

Jade Dragon Freight now ships Lucky Lotus Powder from the Macau Lotus Facility
through the Port of Macau directly to the Golden Lotus Casino in Las Vegas.

Sister Garner manages the Golden Lotus Casino and fulfills orders for the
Kingsley Syndicate on the west side of the Strip.

Bangkok Tuk-Tuk Couriers handle last-mile delivery of Counterfeit Casino Chips
from the Golden Lotus Casino to the Wolfpack.

IMPORTANT UPDATE: the Golden Tiger Warehouse in Bangkok is no longer the origin
of Lucky Lotus Powder. All powder production has moved to the Macau Lotus
Facility. The Port of Bangkok route is discontinued.

Mr. Chow celebrated the expansion by driving a speedboat through the casino
lobby. Attendance was mandatory.
